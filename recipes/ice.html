Ice

Refreshing but difficult dish!

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 ice tray
  • 1 freezer

    Instructions:

  1. Carefully extract water into measuring cup
  2. Deposit water into ice try, ensuring none spills over (very important!)
  3. Carefully place tray inside freezer
  4. After an excrutiating 4-5 hours, remove ice from freezer and enjoy!
