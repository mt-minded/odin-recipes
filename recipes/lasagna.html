Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

    Ingredients:

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 brick mozzarella
  • 12 lasagna noodles

    Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a saucepan until browned. Season to taste
  2. Saute crushed garlic cloves and oregano with ground beef until lightly browned
  3. Mix in tomato sauce with ground beef and let simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, boil lasagna noodles and drain once cooked
  5. Beat together egg and ricotta cheese. In a separate bowl, shred/grate mozzarella
  6. To assemble, place two strips of lasagna noodles in a baking pan. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese ontop of the noodles, followed by a layer of pasta sauce. Place some shredded mozzarella followed by two strips of noodles. Repeat this procedure 3-4 times depending on the height of the pan. When completed, top with a thick layer of mozzarella cheese and some grated parmesan and herbs (optional)
  7. Cover the lasagana with foil and bake at 375 for 40 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes uncovered
  8. Remove from oven, rest for 5-10 minutes, and serve!
